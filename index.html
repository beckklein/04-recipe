Roasted Butternut Squash and Chickpeas with Hot Honey

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2.5 lb butternut squash, skinned and cubed into 1 inch pieces
  • Garam masala to taste
  • Red pepper flakes to taste
  • 5 sprigs fresh thyme
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 0.5 cup fresh cilantro
  • 1-2 tbsp hot honey
  • Plain yogurt or sour cream

Steps:

  1. Drain and rinse chickpeas. Place on a paper towel-lined baking sheet and rest on stovetop. Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Let chickpeas dry as you prepare other ingredients.
  2. Prepare butternut squash. Place cubed squash pieces into a mixing bowl and toss with garam masala, salt, thyme, red-pepper flakes, and two tbsp olive oil. Spread squash onto parchment paper-lined baking sheet and roast for 20 minutes.
  3. Once the squash has roasted for 20 minutes, slice the onion. Repeat seasoning and tossing process with chickpeas and onion together, and place on a separate parchment paper-lined baking sheet. Roast both chickpeas and squash for 30 more minutes.
  4. Remove sheets from oven and combine. Drizzle vinegar and hot honey, garnish with fresh herbs, and serve with yogurt or sour cream if desired.
